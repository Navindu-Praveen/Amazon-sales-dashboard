1. **Card Visuals:**
   - Total Sales (Amount)
   - Total Quantity Sold
   - Number of Orders

2. **Donut Chart:**
   - Order Status Distribution (e.g., Pending, Shipped, Delivered)

3. **Stacked Bar Chart:**
   - Fulfillment Method Distribution
   - Sales Channel Distribution

4. **Map:**
   - Geographic Distribution (Top Shipping Cities, States, Countries)

5. **Bar Chart:**
   - Top Styles in Sales
   - Top Categories in Sales
   - Top Sizes in Sales

6. **Line Chart:**
   - Sales Trend Over Time (Date)

7. **Table:**
   - Detailed Order Information (Order ID, Date, Status, Fulfillment, etc.)

8. **KPI (Key Performance Indicator):**
   - Average Order Value
   - B2B vs. B2C Sales Comparison

9. **Treemap:**
   - Currency Distribution

10. **Stacked Column Chart:**
    - Shipping Service Level Distribution

11. **Doughnut Chart or Stacked Bar Chart:**
    - Courier Status Distribution

12. **Funnel Chart:**
    - Sales Funnel (if applicable)
